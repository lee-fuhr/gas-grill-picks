How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own timed cook-offs or lab tests. Instead, we base every recommendation on a few trusty sources: the long-term feedback of owners who’ve lived with these grills for years, the opinions of certified barbecue technicians who repair them, and published reviews from established outdoor-cooking experts. We also track what actually breaks on popular models, igniters that fizzle out, burner tubes that rust through, and grease trays that are a pain to clean, so you know which grills avoid those headaches. Our methodology is honest about trade-offs. A grill might earn near-perfect marks for durability but lose points for awkward assembly or a cramped cooking area. By weighing these factors against real-world owner experiences, we aim to give you a clear picture of what a grill is like to own, not just unbox.
