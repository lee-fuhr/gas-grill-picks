Best budget gas grills

Quick answer

For most shoppers, the Nexgrill 720-0830H 4-Burner Propane Gas Grill is the best budget gas grill because it hits the sweet spot of cooking space, heat output, and lasting build quality.

The Char-Broil Performance 4-Burner Cabinet is a solid runner-up if you want a tidy cart with extra storage, while the Dyna-Glo DGE486SSP-D is the editors’ pick for those who prioritize raw power and low cost.

Not everyone needs a six-burner beast that costs as much as a used car. A budget-friendly propane gas grill can still deliver juicy burgers, evenly cooked chicken, and that unmistakable grilled flavor without emptying your wallet. Three- and four-burner models in this price bracket are ideal for apartment balconies, small patios, or weekend cooks who don’t grill every night. They’re also smart picks for beginners figuring out their cook style. When shopping on a budget, pay attention to three things: burner count (three burners are fine for most, four give you more indirect cooking room), cooking area (look for at least 400 square inches of primary space), and material quality. At this price point, porcelain-coated steel lids and stainless-steel burners are common. None will outlast a high-end Weber, but the best ones offer consistent heat distribution and easy cleanup for years of regular use.
